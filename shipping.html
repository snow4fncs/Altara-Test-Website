Delivery Rates

We offer a range of shipping options across Australia and internationally. All orders are fulfilled within 1-2 business days of payment confirmation.

Free Shipping Threshold

Complimentary standard shipping applies automatically to all Australian orders. No minimum spend required. Free shipping eligibility is displayed at checkout prior to payment.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees levied by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the purchase price or shipping cost. Altara cannot predict or control these fees.

Order Processing
01
Order Confirmed

You will receive an order confirmation email immediately after payment is processed. This email contains your order number and a summary of your purchase.

02
Fulfilment

Your order enters our fulfilment queue within 2-4 hours of confirmation. Each cover is inspected for quality before packaging. Orders placed before 12:00 PM AEST are typically dispatched the same business day.

03
Dispatched

Once your order has left our facility, you will receive a dispatch confirmation email containing your tracking number. Use this to monitor your shipment via the Australia Post website or the carrier's tracking portal.

04
Delivered

Your Altara seat cover arrives in protective packaging, ready to install. If no one is available to receive the parcel, the carrier will leave a collection card or arrange a redelivery according to their standard procedure.

Important Notes
Business Day Definition

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding Australian public holidays. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next available business day.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your delivery address is entered correctly at checkout. Altara is not responsible for delays or failed deliveries resulting from an incorrect or incomplete address. If you notice an error, contact us immediately after placing your order — changes cannot be made once an order has been dispatched.

Lost or Delayed Parcels

In the rare event your parcel is lost or significantly delayed beyond the estimated timeframe, please contact us. We will liaise with the carrier on your behalf and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
